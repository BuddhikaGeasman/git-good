# Who am I? What do I do?

I've been building things with React for over 10 years, which is basically a lifetime in frontend years. Double majored in electronics and computer science back when that seemed like a good idea. These days I'm a senior frontend engineer at an AI software company, building agentic chat with in-house MCP tools. Before this, I survived herding 10 teams through the beautiful disaster that is micro-frontends and Module Federation—think of it as teaching cats to share toys, except the toys are JavaScript modules and everyone's deploy breaks at 4pm on Friday.

When I'm not debugging Claude's latest adventures, you'll find me reading solarpunk novels (currently *What If We Get It Right?* because the future doesn't have to suck), drawing vis dev work from the game dev career I never quite pursued, or out on the water sculling. I've been rowing for 10+ years, which apparently wasn't enough punishment, so seven months ago I started doing jiujitsu. Turns out "cross-training" is just a fancy word for collecting new ways to be sore. I live in Austin, where the tacos are good and the summer is a war crime.
